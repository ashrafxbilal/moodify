Transform your browsing experience by adapting website colors to match your mood

About Moodify Vibe Color

Moodify Vibe Color is an intelligent browser extension that enhances your web browsing experience by dynamically changing the color scheme of websites based on the mood you select. Whether you're feeling calm, energetic, focused, or relaxed, our extension adapts color palettes to complement your current state of mind.

Key Features

Mood-Based Color Adaptation: Choose from a variety of mood settings that adjust website colors accordingly
Instant Switching: Seamlessly switch between different mood themes with a single click
Website Compatibility: Works on most websites without breaking their functionality
Customization Options: Fine-tune color intensity and contrast to suit your preferences
Easy Toggle: Quickly enable or disable the extension when needed

Quick Start Guide

1 Access the Extension: Click on the Moodify Vibe Color icon in your browser toolbar
2 Select Your Mood: Choose from the mood options (Calm, Energetic, Focused, Relaxed, etc.)
3 Adjust Intensity: Use the sliders to customize the color intensity if needed
4 Apply Changes: Click "Apply" to transform the current webpage
5 Save Preferences: Your settings will be remembered for future browsing sessions

Tips for Best Experience

Website Compatibility: Some websites with strict styling may not fully adapt to color changes. You can disable the extension for specific sites from the options panel.
Performance Optimization: For very large or complex websites, consider reducing the color intensity to improve performance.
Preset Schedules: You can set up scheduled mood changes in the advanced settings - for example, switching to "Focused" mode during work hours.
