Privacy Policy for Moodify Vibe Color

Last Updated: March 23, 2024

Overview

Moodify Vibe Color is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles data and what information we collect, if any.

Data Collection

Moodify Vibe Color is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Local Storage

The extension uses Firefox's local storage to save your preferences, such as:

  • Your chosen mood settings
  • Color intensity preferences
  • Site-specific settings
  • Time-based automation settings

All this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any servers.

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To apply color modifications to the current tab
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • tabs: To manage tab-specific settings
  • *://*/*: To apply color modifications to web pages

These permissions are used only for the core functionality of the extension and are not used for data collection.

Third-Party Services

Moodify Vibe Color does not use any third-party services or analytics tools. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. All your preferences are stored locally on your device using Firefox's built-in storage mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Compliance

This extension complies with Firefox's Add-on Policies and Mozilla's Privacy Principles. We are committed to maintaining transparency about our data practices and protecting user privacy.
